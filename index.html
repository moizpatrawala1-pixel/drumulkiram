FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about visiting Dr Unwala's Dental Clinic. Can't find your answer? WhatsApp us anytime.

How do I book an appointment?
+
The easiest way is to WhatsApp us directly at +91 83697 86201 — we typically respond within a few hours during clinic hours. You can also fill out the contact form on our Contact page and we'll get back to you promptly.
What should I expect at my first consultation?
+
Your first visit includes a comprehensive dental examination where Dr Ummulkiram will assess your overall oral health, discuss any concerns you have, and take X-rays if needed. She'll then walk you through her findings and recommend a treatment plan — clearly explaining all your options without any pressure. First consultations typically last 30–45 minutes.
Is root canal treatment painful?
+
Modern root canal treatment is far more comfortable than its reputation suggests. With today's advanced anaesthetics and rotary techniques, most patients feel no more discomfort than a routine filling. The majority of patients report that the procedure was far easier than they anticipated. Dr Ummulkiram will ensure you're completely comfortable throughout.
Am I a good candidate for Invisalign?
+
Invisalign is suitable for most mild to moderate cases of crowding, spacing, and bite issues in both teens and adults. The best way to find out is through a consultation. Dr Ummulkiram will assess your teeth and let you know whether Invisalign, traditional braces, or another option is most appropriate for your specific case.
How long do dental implants last?
+
With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. The implant itself (the titanium root) is permanent; the crown on top may need replacement after 15–25 years depending on wear. Good oral hygiene and regular check-ups are key to maximising the lifespan of your implants.
Do you treat children?
+
Absolutely. We welcome patients of all ages, including young children. We take a gentle, patient, and positive approach with our younger patients to make dental visits comfortable and even fun. We recommend bringing children in for their first visit around age 2–3 to establish good habits early.
What are your clinic hours?
+
Please WhatsApp us or call directly for our current clinic hours, as these may vary. We're located at Al Saddah Shopping Complex, 1st Floor, F-11, Bhindi Bazaar, Mumbai. We do our best to accommodate patients with busy schedules and can discuss appointment flexibility on request.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept cash, UPI (all major apps), and card payments. For larger treatments like implants, full mouth rehabilitation, or orthodontic treatment, we can discuss payment plans to make your care more accessible. Please speak with us at the time of consultation.
What makes Dr Unwala's Dental Clinic different from other practices?
+
We combine 53 years of trusted community practice with internationally-trained expertise — Dr Ummulkiram completed her Postgraduate Diploma in Restorative Dentistry at Newcastle University, UK. We take time with every patient, provide honest advice (never unnecessary treatments), and treat you in a warm, welcoming environment. We're not a high-volume clinic — we're your family dentist.
