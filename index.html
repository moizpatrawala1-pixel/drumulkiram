Our Story

Founded in 1973. Carried forward today.

Dr. Unwala's Dental Clinic was founded in 1973 by Dr. Yusuf Unwala, a name synonymous with trusted dental care in Bhindi Bazaar. Today, his daughter Dr. Ummulkiram Unwala carries that legacy forward — with international training and a deeply personal touch.

Patients choose us because we take the time to understand each individual's needs, offer honest advice, and deliver results in a calm, welcoming space. Whether it's your child's first visit or a complete smile transformation, you're in the best hands.

Our Journey

A legacy, in stages.

1973
The clinic is founded
Dr. Yusuf Unwala opens his practice in Bhindi Bazaar, bringing modern dental care to the local community for the first time.
1990s
A trusted name across generations
Word of mouth grows the practice into a multi-generational fixture — families return decade after decade.
2010s
Dr. Ummulkiram trains internationally
After completing her BDS, she pursues a PG Diploma in Restorative Dentistry at Newcastle University, UK.
Today
The legacy continues
Dr. Ummulkiram leads the clinic with modern techniques — Invisalign, implants, full mouth rehabilitation — and the same personal warmth the practice was built on.
