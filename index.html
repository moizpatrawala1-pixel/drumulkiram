Her Story

Dentistry Was Always
In Her Blood

Dr. Ummulkiram Unwala grew up watching her grandfather, Dr. Yusuf Unwala, transform the lives of patients one smile at a time. His clinic at Bhindi Bazaar was — and remains — a place where patients felt genuinely cared for, not just treated. From a young age, she knew she wanted to carry that same spirit forward.

After completing her BDS at YMT Dental College, Mumbai, Dr. Ummulkiram went on to pursue a Postgraduate Diploma in Restorative Dentistry at Newcastle University, UK — gaining hands-on training in complex tooth restoration, smile rehabilitation, and aesthetic dentistry at one of Europe's leading dental schools.

She returned to Mumbai with a broader clinical perspective and a deeper commitment to the values her grandfather instilled: patience, honesty, and care that never feels rushed. Dr. Yusuf continues to be a presence at the clinic — offering guidance and the wisdom of over five decades in practice — while Dr. Ummulkiram leads the day-to-day clinical work.

"Every patient walks in with a story. I take the time to listen before I ever pick up an instrument."

Modern root canal treatment is performed under local anaesthesia and is generally no more uncomfortable than a routine filling. Most patients are surprised by how painless the procedure is. Any mild soreness after the procedure typically resolves within a day or two.
Invisalign works well for most mild to moderate alignment issues — crowding, spacing, overbite, underbite, and crossbite. Complex cases may be better suited to traditional braces. A consultation with Dr. Unwala will determine the most effective treatment for your specific case.
The implant itself is placed in a single appointment (1–2 hours). Healing typically takes 3–6 months before the permanent crown is fitted. We monitor progress carefully throughout and will guide you at each stage.
We recommend bringing children for their first visit around age 2–3, or as soon as their first teeth appear. Early visits help children become comfortable with dental care and allow us to monitor development from the start.
We recommend a check-up and professional cleaning every 6 months for most patients. Those with a higher risk of gum disease or cavities may benefit from more frequent visits. Regular check-ups catch problems early — before they become costly or complex.
We welcome walk-in patients, though appointments are preferred to minimise your waiting time. You can easily book via WhatsApp — we typically respond within the hour during clinic hours.
Full mouth rehabilitation is a comprehensive treatment plan that addresses multiple dental issues simultaneously — combining crowns, bridges, implants, dentures, and other restorations to restore health, function, and aesthetics to the entire mouth. Dr. Unwala has extensive experience with complex FMR cases.
